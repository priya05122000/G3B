Why Should You Study in Germany?

  • Low Tuition Fees: Public colleges frequently offer tuition-free education to international students.
  • Top Ranked Universities: Germany is home to globally recognized universities with English-taught courses.
  • Reasonable Cost of Living: Living costs are lower compared to other Western countries.
  • Scholarships: Many funding options are available for tuition and living expenses.
  • Work While Studying: International students can work part-time during studies.

Scholarships Offered in Germany

  • Expatrio Scholarship: Up to €15,000 for international students based on a video application.
  • DAAD Scholarship Programs: Fully funded with monthly stipends of €992 (graduates) and €1,300 (PhD).
  • Heinrich Böll Foundation Scholarships: Open to Master's and PhD students in all fields.
  • Erasmus+ Scholarship Programs: For students in short-cycle to doctoral programs in exchange programs.
  • StipendiumPlus: Includes base stipend up to €855, health insurance, child support, and study expenses.
