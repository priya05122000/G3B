Test Preparations

We provide expert coaching for a wide range of standardized tests, including:

  • IELTS (International English Language Testing System)
  • TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language)
  • GRE (Graduate Record Examinations)
  • GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test)
  • SAT (Scholastic Assessment Test)
  • Duolingo English Test
  • PTE (Pearson Test of English)

Our training includes in-depth lessons on test formats, strategies for each section, time management techniques, and targeted practice sessions.
