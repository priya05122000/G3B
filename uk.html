Why Should You Study in the UK?

  • Excellent Education: The United Kingdom has the second-best higher education system in the world. The Research Excellence Framework states that over 80–82% of research conducted by UK institutions is considered "globally exceptional."
  • Reputed Institutions: As per the QS World University Rankings 2025, the UK is home to four of the world’s top ten universities.
  • Multifarious Course Offerings: UK universities offer over 1,000 courses across more than 20 subjects, giving students the opportunity to specialize early.
  • Short Duration of Degree: UK degrees are more affordable due to their shorter duration. Undergraduate degrees typically take 3 years, and postgraduate degrees usually only take 1 year.
  • Part-Time Job Opportunities: International students can work 18–20 hours per week during term and full-time during holidays.
